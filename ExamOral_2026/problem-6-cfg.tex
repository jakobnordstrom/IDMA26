  \frametitle{Context-Free Grammars}


  Let $a, b, c, d, e$ be terminals; $S, T$ be non-terminals; and $S$
  be the starting symbol in the grammar
  \begin{align*}
    S &\produces aSe \\
    S &\produces T \\
    T &\produces bT \\
    T &\produces cdT \\
    T &\produces 
  \end{align*}

%     \blankline

  Which of the following strings can or cannot be generated by this grammar? Why?
  \begin{enumerate}
  \item 
    $aaabcdbcdeee$

  \item 
    $aabbeeee$

  \item 
    $aadcbdcbee$

  \item 
    $abbbbcde$

  \item
    $abbeacde$

  \end{enumerate}


\end{frame}
